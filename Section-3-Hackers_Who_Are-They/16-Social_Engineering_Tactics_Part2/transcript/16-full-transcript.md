## Lunch Time Attack

So, the first technique in here is something  
that we call the lunch time attack.

Employee does not log off  
before leaving the workstation for lunch.

Now, this probably happens at your place of work,  
where you have Bob or Nancy,  
they're logged in, and they're working on some stuff,  
and then they see, oh, it's lunchtime,  
and then they just stand up and then they walk away  
to go have lunch, without logging out.

This is particularly dangerous,  
because if you have an insider,  
someone who already works for your company,  
who has negative intentions against the company,  
and maybe Nancy or Bob was actually working  
on something very, very important,  
this might be the opportunity for them to actually  
go over to the computer, and see what they're working on.

This is a classic type of attack,  
and, obviously, you need to be aware of this.

If you are going for lunch,  
please do log out of your work station.
